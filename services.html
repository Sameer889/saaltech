Big idea

The concept of advertising a brand by providing a long, boring list of benefits to the audience has become way too orthodox. People don’t want brands to straight up sell themselves anymore. They want a story, a perspective - something that will stimulate their minds, elicit emotions and make them go ‘WOAH’. Our team of creative gurus leverage their insights to curate compelling & creative campaigns that revolve around a single ‘big idea.’ Campaigns that Ll make your target audience stop right in their footsteps and think. Really, think.

2D/3D Animation

Animation remains an impactful medium to utilize for any company, whether it's for social or as a cost-effective approach to communicate a brand narrative. From the conceptualization of the animation to the design of the characters, Saaltech provides you with a ridiculously talented lot of graphic designers and 2D and 3D animators that use dynamic motion graphics to elevate just about any content you give! Apart from compelling 2D animations, we also make some pretty amazing three-dimensional advertising animations.

Digital Marketing

TVC/DVC Production

Brand Idea Development

Your brand might already have a set personality that people know it for, or maybe you have a brief idea but can't seem to pen it down. We assist you in developing a distinct brand identity that sets your company apart from the competition. The process includes defining a brand's mission, values, guidelines, visual identity, strategy and voice, as well as how it communicates its promise to the target audience.

Mobile Applications

Whether it is a consumer-oriented app or a game-changing business solution, our professionals handle the complete cycle of mobile app development, including business analysis, UX/UI design, AND development of your mobile app from concept to launch, as well as integration of the new product into your infrastructure & on-demand optimization and scale-up.

Annual Report

Our annual reports feature data and performance insights from all marketing channels we've utilized to promote your brand, goods, & services. This allows us and your brand to see if goals and KPIs are being fulfilled, which social media initiatives are working and which aren't, as well as financial data.
